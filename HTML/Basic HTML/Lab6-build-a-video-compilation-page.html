Rhythm Game

Rhythm game is a genre of game where you play the while reacting to the song or music. Usually the game mechanic is there is a note falling down and you have click when the note touches the judgement line. The number of notes and timing of it touching the judgement line is according to the music rhythm. In this page I will introduce 3 rhythm games that I usually plays and video of gameplay of my favourite in the said game.

Maimai

Maimai is a arcade rhythm game developed by Sega and it has a unique mechanics among rhythm games wheres you play at a huge machine that looks like washing machine and you play by tapping the button and occasionally the screen depending on the notes type.

Arcaea

Arcaea is a mobile rhythm game developed by lowiro. This game also also has unique mechanism as it has a 3D like judgement line making one of the challenging rhythm game. (It's a very interesting game give it a try)

Project Sekai

Project Sekai is a mobile rhythm game that's also developed by Sega. It's game mechanism is like your typical game but since its mobile you tap on the notes by tapping your mobile devicecs screen. This game appeals to people who likes vocaloid songs since it does have lots of vocaloid song.
