Sushi Rice

Welcome to the ultimate guide for making your own sushi rice! These sushi rice are perfect for satisfying your craving of japanese food in your own country. Follow this simple recipe to create delicious sushi rice that youcan use to make sushi.

Ingredients

  • rice vinegar
  • sugar
  • salt
  • freshly-cooked rice

Instructions

  1. Transfer the freshly cooked rice to a sushi bucket (or bowl) and spread the sushi vinegar all over it, making sure to distribute it through the rice scoop.
  2. At first, move the rice scoop widely and mix from the bottom to thoroughly mix the sushi vinegar and rice.
  3. Once the rice and sushi vinegar have been mixed to a certain extent, move the rice spoon in small, horizontal motions to remove any lumps in the rice .
  4. Spread the sushi rice out and let it cool using a fan. Once the surface has cooled down, turn it upside down with a rice scoop and let it cool until the whole part reaches the temperature of human skin.
  5. Once the sushi rice has reached the consistency of human skin, cover it loosely with a wet cloth or plastic wrap and wait for the sushi rice to serve! (*If you put it in the refrigerator, the sushi rice will become hard, so store it at room temperature!)
